Full Instructions

Many streams may not play due to CORS restrictions. Install this browser extension to bypass the restriction (Highly Recommended):

  • Chrome: Allow CORS: Access-Control-Allow-Origin
  • Firefox: Allow CORS: Access-Control-Allow-Origin


Remember to click the icon for the extension and turn it on



Alternatively, you can install this browser extension to play the video directly and choose the channel's "Direct" link:

  • Chrome: Native MPEG-Dash + HLS Playback
  • Firefox: Native MPEG-Dash + HLS Playback


Lastly, you can copy the Direct link and paste it into a video player like VLC as a network stream.
